Flavor Descriptions

Cookies and Cream

Calling all Oreo cookie lovers! This treat has Oreo cookie crumbles mixed into the vanilla cake batter and the french vanilla buttercream as well. A classic Oreo cookie tops this cake -- we'll let you decide which part to eat first.

Birthday Cake

Enjoy this treat on your special day, or any day this season! Vanilla cake is mixed with colorful sprinkles and topped with french vanilla buttercream. We top it off with more sprinkles, of course!

Cinna-Pumpkin

Exclusively available during the fall, this pumpkin cupcake is sure to be a favorite! We use Eddie's family recipe for pumpkin sweet bread and top it with cinnamon buttercream. Simple, but oh so delicious.

Hummingbird Cake

Try this classic Southern recipe in cupcake form. The cake has pineapple, walnuts, cinnamon, and bananas. The cream cheese is literally the frosting on this wonderfully tasty cake.
